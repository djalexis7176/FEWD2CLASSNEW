Hamsters

Hamsters are small, nocturnal rodents known for their playful nature and low-maintenance care. Popular as pets, they come in various breeds, with Syrian and Dwarf hamsters being the most common. They love burrowing, running on wheels, and storing food in their cheek pouches.

To stay healthy, hamsters need a balanced diet, a spacious cage with enrichment, and gentle handling. They are most active at dawn and dusk, making them ideal pets for evening interaction. With proper care, they can live 2–3 years, bringing joy to their owners.
